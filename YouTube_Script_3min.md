## [0:20 - 0:50] THE VISION (What We Built)

That's why I built **MedGuidance AI**—an evidence-based medical assistant that never fabricates references.

Think of it as two specialized tools in one:

**For healthcare professionals**: A clinical research copilot that searches 57 medical databases simultaneously—PubMed, Cochrane, WHO, CDC, FDA—and delivers answers with verified citations in seconds. Every claim backed by real research papers, real guidelines, real evidence.

**For everyone else**: A consumer-friendly health companion that translates complex medical information into simple, actionable advice. No jargon. No fear-mongering. Just clear guidance on what's happening, what you can do, and when to see a doctor.

---

## [0:50 - 1:30] HOW IT WORKS (The Magic)

Here's what makes MedGuidance different:

**Evidence-only architecture**. We disabled Google Search entirely. The system only uses curated medical databases—57 of them—queried in parallel. That means comprehensive, accurate answers in 5 to 7 seconds.

**Zero hallucinated citations**. Every reference is validated server-side against our evidence database. If it's not in PubMed, Cochrane, or an official guideline, it doesn't get cited. Period.

**Medical image analysis**. Upload an X-ray, CT scan, or MRI. Our advanced vision system—with 93% accuracy—identifies findings, highlights regions of interest, and provides evidence-based differential diagnoses.

**Privacy-first design**. No server-side storage. Your conversations expire after one hour. No tracking. No data collection. Your health information stays yours.
